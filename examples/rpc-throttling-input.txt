Incident report:

During a claims campaign, users intermittently see pending transactions for 90-120 seconds.
About 24% of claim attempts fail, mostly from mobile wallets.

Observed logs:

sendRawTransaction returned 429 Too Many Requests from the primary RPC.
Several requests returned 503 Service Unavailable.
TransactionExpiredBlockheightExceededError after wallet approval.
Provider dashboard says "Node is behind" and websocket subscriptions reconnect every few minutes.

Recent changes:

- Write traffic moved to the same RPC key used for indexer backfills.
- Backend retry count increased from 2 to 8.
